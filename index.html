Machinery for the Fresh Produce Industry

Tri-Pak Machinery, Inc. designs and builds high quality, custom packing house equipment for the fresh produce industry, as well as an extensive line of industrial ice crushers and other customized manufacturing solutions.

Located in South Texas, Tri-Pak is a family run business focused on providing solutions to maximize productivity in the modern packing house. The company continues to develop better and more efficient machinery, utilizing the newest technology, while keeping the tradition of quality and consistency that began more than 80 years ago.
